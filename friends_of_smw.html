Friends of St Margaret Ward

Our'Friends of St Margaret Ward School' fundraising group, organise various events and activities throughout the year.
Anyone can be a 'friend' of our school - just come along to any meetings you can manage and perhaps help out at any events you attend. We appreciate any offers of help whether it be setting up tables and chairs; selling tickets; donating prizes; helping in the kitchen or clearing things away.
