BOOK DAY

World Book Day 2013 - The joy of books and the pleasure of reading was once again celebrated on World Book Day 2013 on Thursday 7 March. All staff contributed to the enjoyment of the day by dressing up as animals! The children came in to school in their pyjamas and explored bedtime stories through short presentations, art and drama.

World Book Day 2012 - To celebrate World Book Day 2012 on Thursday 1 March, our children wore either their pyjamas or character costumes and brought in their favourite stories.
