International Food Festival
A highly successful 'International Food Evening' took place at school on the evening of Wednesday 13 October 2011 when parents, children and friends came along to sample a huge range of food and drink from 12 countries worldwide : India, Colombia, Jamaica, Spain, Portugal, France, England, Ireland, Italy, Greece, Poland and Mexico.

Prior to the event, our school children had spent an afternoon preparing colourful bunting, posters, maps and hats to contribute to the inter-cultural flavour of the evening and their handmade decorations looked wonderful around the hall and stalls.
The food was prepared, presented and sold through the stalls by a hard working group of talented parents and staff who generously volunteered their time, cooking and presentational skills.

The hungry customers were certainly spoilt for choice with the vast array of tempting tastes on every stall.

In addition to a wide variety of food, the stalls also offered the chance to sample drinks, juices and liqueurs from around the world.

Our Festival of World Food proved to be not only another successful social event but also a wonderful way to celebrate the many nationalities and cultures within our school family. Many parents and children commented on how much they had enjoyed the evening and hope that we will make it a regular event.
