St Margaret Ward RC Primary School

Equality Objective;

Why

At St Margaret Ward School in 2011 there was a larger (than National Statistics) gap between the number of boys 56% and the number of girls 33% who failed to achieve Level 4 or above for writing in KS2 2011 SATs.

How

Consult with stakeholders and seek their opinions on how to improve the performance of boys. Explore best practice in schools in our cluster and via Elmridge partnership.

Outcome

An increased level of boys achieving Level 4 or above in writing to 75%.
